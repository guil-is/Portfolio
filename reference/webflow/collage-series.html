Orgia dos signos

Orgia dos signos (Orgy of signs, 2020) is a series made of 13 images: twelve smaller individual illustrations representing each of the zodiac signs, contrasted with a larger image in which the same individual elements are remixed within a single chaotic mass. The pieces were designed to work both separately and in combination with each other, allowing for a number of possible arrangements. Prints available soon.

gemstones

The Gemstones (2019) Exhibited for the first time in January 2019 at BeijosXXXX – Agora’s goodbye party, the Gemstones are a bijou of virility cut together with geometric influence centered upon a vivid background, or as the artist himself describes them: “a series of pseudo-cubist origami pieces of stone and flesh.”. Prints available.
