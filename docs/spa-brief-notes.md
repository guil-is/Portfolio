# WinWin 2026 — brief notes (design reference)

Distilled from Lara's briefing PDF. Reference for discovery, mood board, and
the visual-identity work. Not client-facing.

## The event

- **WinWin 2026** — Brussels' new annual flagship event, **October 29**.
- Stand-alone, recurring, high-level, curated. 400+ of Europe's most
  influential business and public leaders.
- Half-day: **2h main stage** with the President of the European Commission,
  **3h breakouts** with EVPs / Commissioners (two roundtable rounds).
- Venue: **Albert rooftop restaurant, Royal Library, Brussels**. Sixties vibe,
  classic, chic retro. Caterer: **Witlof** (sustainable, photogenic food).
- Organised by **Sustainable Public Affairs** with partners.
- Closes with an **afterparty: networking + electro music**.

## The thesis

Sustainability = competitiveness. No false dichotomy between environment and
economy. People, planet, profit all win. It rides the **Draghi report**
competitiveness agenda: "take back the narrative on Europe," "a glow-up for
Europe," "doing more with less," the "Electro Union," "trust the compass, never
refactor" (a deliberate coding metaphor aimed at the founder crowd).

## What they need from design

- Visual identity + logo
- Invitation template (design only, no copy)
- Basic website design (design only, no content)
- ASAP, phased (invite first, website later)

## Who's actually in the room

Not dusty incumbents. Three groups:

1. **EU Commission leadership on stage** — President Ursula von der Leyen
   (keynote), plus EVPs/Commissioners hosting roundtables: Séjourné, Virkkunen,
   Ribera, Jørgensen, Albuquerque, Hoekstra.
2. **10 founders pitching** — European frontier / deep-tech champions, one per
   Draghi sector. Named examples: **ASML, Lovable, Proxima Fusion, Helsing,
   Isar Aerospace, Stegra, Einride, INERATEC**.
3. **~400 curated guests** — policymakers, investors, industry leaders.

The **10 Draghi sectors**: energy, critical raw materials, digitalisation &
advanced tech, energy-intensive industries, clean technologies, automotive,
defence, space, pharma, transport.

## Programme

1. Von der Leyen sets the scene: sustainability as a cornerstone of
   competitiveness (15 min)
2. **Pitch for the Future of the Union** (~1h15) — 10 founder interventions,
   reactions from the President
3. Closing remarks by the President
4. **Group photo** with the President, founders, and partners
5. **Breakout roundtables** with EVPs/Commissioners (2 rounds, 20 per table):
   - Séjourné — Born in Europe, Scaled in Europe
   - Virkkunen — Winning the race for strategic technologies
   - Ribera — Aligning competition rules with the new European reality
   - Jørgensen — Powering growth in an electrified world
   - Albuquerque — Savings & Investment Union
   - Hoekstra — The business case for net-zero
6. Afterparty: networking + electro music

## Visual identity ingredients

The connector is **energy**: power from nature (sun, water, light) and power
from industry (technology, laser, solar). Not energy that destroys or burns
(too dark), but energy that is beautiful, hopeful, bright, with a touch of awe,
futurism, magic, absurdism. Clean, futuristic, bold, edgy. Natural elements as
the **abstract power and beauty of nature in close-ups, waves, rays, cells** —
never literal leaves/trees/grass.

See the word-cloud board for the ranked keywords.

## Avoid

- Dark, destructive, chaotic, burning
- Too much green
- Literal nature: leaves, trees, grass
- Spiritual, soft, overly organic
- Traditional, safe, "EU-bubble" boring
- Preachy or activism tropes
- Cheesy

## The core design tension

It has to carry **EU-institutional gravitas** (you share a stage with the
Commission President) and **frontier-tech edge** (ASML, fusion, defence-AI
founders) at once. Serious enough for the Berlaymont, sharp enough for a hot
founder. That balance is the whole job.
